Camp Bali Hati Senior High School

Camp is a school program designed to help students develop character, teamwork, and confidence through fun and meaningful activities.

Our Camps

Funtastic Camp

Funtastic Camp is a fun and engaging school program designed to develop students’ character, creativity, and teamwork through interactive games and group activities. This program creates a joyful learning atmosphere where students can build confidence, strengthen friendships, and enjoy meaningful experiences together.

Survival Camp

Survival Camp is a challenging outdoor program focused on building students’ independence, resilience, and problem-solving skills. Through survival-based activities and teamwork challenges, students learn how to adapt, cooperate, and face obstacles responsibly in a safe and guided environment.

Our Activities

Cooking Competition

The Cooking Competition is a fun activity where students work together to create creative dishes. It builds teamwork and confidence.

Fire Dance

Fire Dance is an energetic performance activity that develops confidence and coordination. Teamwork is essential.

Trekking

Trekking is an outdoor activity that trains endurance and resilience. It encourages teamwork and appreciation for nature.
